About


Hi, I'm a graphic designer with a background in branding, video, exhibit and interactive design. I also have a strong passion for pottery, and have spent the past ten years forming my own practice. Currenlty, I am interested in exploring people's relationships to digital technology, objects, crafts, spaces and their impact on our identity and culture. Through working with speculative designs, interventions, and storytelling, I hope to generate curiousity and alternative perspective to our current situations.
