About


Hello, I'm a multidisciplinary designer with experience across branding, digital, exhibit, and interactive design. I have a passion for traditional crafts and have also been working in pottery for the past decade. I am constantly looking for ways to combine my interests and broaden my perspectives.

Currently, I'm interested in exploring our relationships with digital technology, objects, crafts, spaces and their impact on our identity and culture. I aspire to learn more about creative coding, machine learning, robotics, bio materials, and chemistry. And with these tools, I hope to generate curiousity and alternate perspectives through speculative design, interventions, and object storytelling.
