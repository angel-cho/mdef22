Process Experiments


Barcelona, Spain, 2021

Found in Translation

Like spoken language, written language comes in many different shapes and expressions. Can AI detect the nuances of written language? What does it mean when I can translate some things but not others? Does this change and limit the ways we are able to express ourselves through written language? As AI and AR technology becomes more commonplace in our daily live, how would these biases affect the decisions we make and the experiences we have?

Taken to the extreme: graffiti is a form of written expression typically using stylized lettering. Often associated with gangsters, outlaws, and criminal activity,... What happens when Google Lens is used to translate graffiti in Barcelona? Is the graffiti even readable on its own or cary any meaning? Does it even matter? Sometimes the purpose of graffiti is just to exist and be seen in a public space. Visibility before readability. So, I know that I won't get a direct translation of words from this experiment, but what will I get out of it?

Translating abstract expression into abstract words in standardized fonts through AI and AR.
