Micro Challenge 1

For this first micro challenge, I teamed up with Vikrant and Busi. Upon our initial ven diagram exercise of plotting our interests, we found our common interests in how design affect our behavior and culture, creative and tangible ways to visualize data, storytelling and indigenous practices, and gamification. From here, we naturally came upon the idea of creating a game that uses indigenous (non-western) practices as a key element. We also wanted the game to be useful to our personal and collective experiences in some form. So we decided to create a game that aids collaborative idea generation amongst MDEF students and other designers and makers.
