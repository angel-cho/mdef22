Principles & Practices

Our first Fab Academy class was a recap of web and git basics, which we covered in the beginning of the first term as we were tasked to create and upload our own websites. We discussed the history of git and the reason behind the usage of it, mainly focused around version control, and were shown examples on best practices and ways to use git effectively for collaborations. We also briefly went over different coding languages to build websites including html and markdown. Finally, we discussed the structure of the fab academy course and what to expect on a weekly basis.
